About

As regular news readers we at Good News have noticed that a good majority of news found on popular news sites is negative in nature. We spoke to journalists about potential reasoning behind this and were assured that "blood sells". While this may be true we would like to provide an alternative to people who are not in the mood for negativity.

Patrick and Ben, the founders of GoodNews, met at a Holberton School and decided to bring their passion for goodness to the public domain when tasked to work on a final school project.
